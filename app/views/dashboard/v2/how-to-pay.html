How to pay a CCJ

Contact the claimant and agree how to pay them - for example, cheque or direct debit.

Make sure you keep records for all payments made. You’ll need proof of your payments if there’s any disagreement.

Use the claim number and claim reference on any payment you make. You should use the claim reference and claim number on a:

  • bank transfer
  • receipt you get from the claimant if you pay by cheque

You should allow at least 4 days for your payment to reach the claimant. Don’t bring or send payments to the court - they won’t be accepted.
