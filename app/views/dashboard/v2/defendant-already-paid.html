Completing your claim

Make sure you can prove you’ve paid. Send a cheque or postal order by post, or make a bank transfer. Don’t send cash through the post.

If you paid the claim amount on or before 1 October 2017 you can apply have the judgment cancelled. If approved the CCJ will be removed from the register.

If you paid the claim amount on or after 2 October 2017 you can apply have the judgment satisfied . If approved the CCJ will remain on the register for 6 years but be marked as satisfied. This will make it easier to apply for credit.

You'll have to pay a court fee of £15.
