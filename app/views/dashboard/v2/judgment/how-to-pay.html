How to pay a County Court Judgment (CCJ)

Contact the claimant and agree how to pay. For example by cheque or direct debit.

Use the claim number for payments such as:

  • bank transfers
  • receipts you get from the claimant if you pay by cheque

Make sure you keep a record of all payments made

Allow at least 4 days for your payment to reach the claimant.

Don’t bring or send payments to the court - they won’t be accepted.
