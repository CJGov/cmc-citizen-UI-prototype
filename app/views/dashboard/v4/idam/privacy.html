Privacy policy

When you use the money claims service we will ask you for some personal data. This privacy policy explains why we collect your personal data, what we do with it and your rights.

The money claim service is managed by Her Majesty’s Courts and Tribunals Service and will be referred to as we from now on.

More information about how to use the service is in the terms and conditions.

Why we need your personal data

We collect your personal data to process your application and to meet legal requirements. We also collect data to improve the service.

The personal data we need to collect includes:

  • your name, address and contact details
  • information that tells us if you open an email from us, or click on a link in an email
  • information about how you use this service, like your IP address and the web browser you use. We do this by using cookies.

We are known as the ‘controllers’ of the personal data you provide.

What we do with your data

HMCTS is an executive agency of the Ministry of Justice and their personal information charter explains how we process data.

Your personal data is processed by our staff in the UK, and the data is stored in the UK.

We use GOV.UK Notify to send emails. These are processed within the EEA until the point where emails are handed over to your email provider.

How long we keep your data

Your data is stored for one month and is then deleted.

What we would also like to do with your data

As part of your claim we’ll ask if you want to receive notifications to let you know how your application is proceeding. We’ll ask for your name and preferred contact details.

This information is not shared with third parties and you can unsubscribe at any time by phoning 0203 123 1234 or emailing mydata@hmcts.net.

Your rights

You can ask:

  • to see the personal data that we hold on you
  • to have the personal data corrected, removed or deleted
  • that access to this data is restricted (for example made temporarily unavailable)
  • that we hold your data for longer

Disclaimer

While we make every effort to keep GOV.UK up to date, we don’t provide any guarantees, conditions or warranties that the information will be:

  • current
  • secure
  • accurate
  • complete
  • free from bugs or viruses

Email mydata@hmcts.net if you want to see any of this information.

How to complain

If you want to complain about how we have handled your personal data email mydata@hmcts.net.

You can complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office if you:

  • are unsatisfied with our response
  • believe we are not legally processing your personal data
  • accurate
  • complete
  • free from bugs or viruses
