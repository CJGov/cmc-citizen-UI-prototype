Make a money claim

You can make a money claim if a person or organisation owes you money and won’t pay you back.

Before claiming you must contact the person or organisation to try to find a solution. Find out how you can resolve a claim by discussion or mediation.

Your claim, including your name and address, will be sent to the person you say owes you money. They'll get a chance to respond to your claim.

You might have to go to a hearing with a judge if the person says they don’t owe you money.

What you'll need

To use this service you need:

  • current address and contact details for the person or organisation you’re claiming against
  • a credit or debit card to pay the claim fee

Your claim must be for £10,000 or less.

Claim by paper

You can also print and complete a paper claim form. Send it and a cheque for your claim fee to the address on the form.
