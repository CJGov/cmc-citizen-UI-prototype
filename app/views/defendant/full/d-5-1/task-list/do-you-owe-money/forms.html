Defend now and also make a counterclaim

When you submit your response you'll be asked to complete a defence and counterclaim OCPN9B form (opens in a new window) and email it to the Civil Money Courts

On the form you'll be asked to pay a court fee as part of your counterclaim.
Find out more about court fees.

{% elseif data.radio_select_group=="all"%}

I admit all of the claim

When respond you'll be asked to complete an admission OCPN9A form (opens in a new window) and email to the Civil Money Courts

On the form you'll need to state when you’re going to pay the money.
You can suggest a repayment plan or a date when you can pay the money.

{% elseif data.radio_select_group=="amount_less_claimed"%}

Less than the amount claimed

When you submit your response you'll be asked to complete both the admission OCPN9A and a defence and counterclaim OCPN9B forms (forms open in a new window) and email them to the Civil Money Courts

On the form your can offer the claimant the amount of money you believe you owe and explain why you don't owe the remaining amount.
