Make an offer to settle out of court

You could settle this money claim without going to court if you make an offer and reach an agreement.

If your offer is accepted and you settle the claim, then it’s confidential. This means there won’t be a public record of the claim made against you and you won’t get a County Court Judgment.

If the claimant accepts your offer then it’s legally enforceable. This means you must do what you’ve agreed or the claimant can ask the court to make a County Court Judgment against you.

If the claimant rejects your offer they can’t bring it up if you go to a court hearing.
