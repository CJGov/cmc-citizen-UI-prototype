How to cancel a judgment

The court will usually only cancel a judgment if it accepts you didn't know about the claim at the time it was issued.

To apply to cancel a judgment you must follow these steps:

  1. Explain why you think the judgment should be cancelled
  2. Pay a fee of £255
  3. Submit a defence to the original claim
  4. Go to a hearing about the judgment, in most cases

If the court cancels the judgment

We'll send your claim defence to Sam Clark and ask them to respond.

You might have to go to another hearing, about the claim itself.

If your request is rejected

Your defence will be cancelled. You'll have to pay according to the terms of the judgment.

If you decide not to apply

You can pay a £50 fee to ask to change the judgment's payment terms.

You'll still owe the full claim amount, but you can ask to pay at a later date or in instalments.
