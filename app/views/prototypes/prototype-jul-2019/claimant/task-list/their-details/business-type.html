What kind of business is it?

I need help finding out what kind of business it is

Follow these steps:

  1. Ask the business
  2. Find the business name.
  3. Use the business name to help decide
  4. Don't assume lone tradespeople are sole traders or self-employed

Ask the business

Talk to the person you dealt with or contact the business and ask if they're a registered company, a sole trader or self-employed.

Find the business name

Check paperwork like receipts and invoices, or the terms and conditions on their website.

Look at small print or footers - the official name may be there instead of in logos or branding.

Use the business name to help decide

Look for 'ltd' or 'limited' in the business name - this means it's a registered company.

You can also search Companies House to see if a business is a registered company - check the listed employees and address to make sure it's the right business.

Don't assume lone tradespeople are sole traders or self-employed

Check their business name or find them on Companies House first.

If the name doesn't have 'limited' or 'ltd' in it and you can't find it on Companies House, it's likely they're a sole trader or self-employed.
