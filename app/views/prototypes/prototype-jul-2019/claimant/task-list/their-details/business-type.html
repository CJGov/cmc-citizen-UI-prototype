What kind of business is it?

I need help working out what kind of business it is

A single person, such as a tradesman, could be a sole trader or a registered company.

To find out, look at the business's name on paperwork such as receipts, invoices or on their terms and conditions on their website.

The official name is often in smallprint and footers rather than being the name shown on a logo.

A registered company's name will end in 'ltd' or 'limited'.

If in doubt, search Companies House to see if the business is a registred company - check the listed employees and address to make sure it's the company you are looking for.
