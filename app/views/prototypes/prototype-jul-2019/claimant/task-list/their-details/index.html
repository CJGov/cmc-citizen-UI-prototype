Are you sure you have the right defendant details?

Every single detail you give us must be correct, otherwise your claim could fail.

You need to know:

  • their trading status - check if you're claiming against an individual, a sole trader or a company
  • the correct names of any individuals - make sure you spell all names correctly and don't use nicknames or similar
  • the full legal name of any company - this can often be different from the name they trade as or the name you see on ads or logos
  • the right home or registered address - for companies, especially chains, this is unlikely to be the address of a branch you visited

How to get the right details

You can speak to the defendant and ask them to send you details in writing.

Otherwise, we can help you.
