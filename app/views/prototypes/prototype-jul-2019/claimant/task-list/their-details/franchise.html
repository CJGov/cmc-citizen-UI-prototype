Is the business a franchise?

Find out what a franchise is

A franchise is an individual store that has a different owner to the chain it's part of.

KFC, McDonalds, Subway, Costa Coffee and Starbucks are all franchises.

This means one KFC branch may be owned by 'Kentucky Fried Chicken (Great Britain) Limited' and another by a franchisee, 'ABC Food Ltd'.

Use the franchise operator's business name to make your claim - find it on a receipt or ask them
