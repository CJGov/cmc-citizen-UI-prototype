Going through security at a court building

Make sure you check the list of items you can't bring through security: https://www.gov.uk/entering-court-or-tribunal-building.

The list includes everyday items like full-length umbrellas, knitting needles or liquids in glass bottles.

You'll have to leave these items with security and collect them on your way out, so it'll be quicker if you bring as few as possible.

At the court

Follow these steps:

  1. Report to the receptionist or the court usher.
  2. Tell them your case number.
  3. Wait until someone calls your name.
