Help paying your court fee

You might be able to get money off your court fee if you have little or no savings, are on certain benefits or have a low income.

If you apply for help, you'll need to complete a paper form to ask the court to change when you pay the claimant.

You can continue your application online instead. You'll have to pay the full fee.

How to ask for help with the fee

  1. Check whether you're eligible for help
  2. Get a reference number
  3. Complete a paper form, and include your reference number
  4. Send us the form and wait to hear from us

Check eligibility and get a reference number

Tell us about your income and savings (opens in a new window) to get a reference number. You should read the information on whether you're eligible first.

When asked what number is on your court form, enter N245.

You'll get a Help with Fees reference number like this: HWF-ASL-LD2.

Complete the form and send it to us

Download form N245 (opens in a new window).

Enter your Help with Fees reference number in the upper-right of the form.

Post the completed form to:

County Court Business Centre
St Katharine's House
21-27 St Katharine's Street
Northampton
Northamptonshire
NN1 2LH

What happens next?

We'll review your application. This usually takes no longer than 21 days.

We'll contact you by post if your application is unsuccessful or if we need more information.

If your application is successful the court will review your request to change when you pay the claimant.
