Do you want to provide documents to support this application?

You can provide evidence to support your application such as witness statements or a statement of case. All witness statements or statements of case you upload must include the following statement of truth signed by them.

What type of evidence could I use?

These could include:

  • a witness statement
  • a statement of case
  • any other evidence relating to the case

If you upload a witness statement or statement of case from someone else, it must include a statement of truth signed by them, in this format:

I believe that the facts included in this document are true.

I understand that proceedings for contempt of court may be brought against anyone who makes, or causes to be made, a false statement in a document verified by a statement of truth without an honest belief in its truth.


Signed     ........................
