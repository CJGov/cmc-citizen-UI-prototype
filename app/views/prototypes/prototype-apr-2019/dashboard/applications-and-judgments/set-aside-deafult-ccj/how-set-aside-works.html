Remove your CCJ and respond to the claim

Requesting a judge to remove the CCJ has a £255 fee. This process is called 'setting aside' the CCJ

As part of the process you'll need to submit evidence and probably go to a hearing. You'll still have the CCJ until the hearing.

If your request is successful

The will CCJ removed and you'll have to respond to the claim against you.

You probably won't get the £255 fee back.

If your request isn't successful

You will have to pay Mrs Jan Clark the full £1500 in the claim.

You won't get the £255 fee back and your CCJ will remain for 6 years.
