How mediation works

The mediation service will contact you and Mary Richards to arrange an appointment with a mediator.

You'll talk to the mediator one-on-one - you won't talk to Mary Richards directly. The call will last up to an hour.

The mediator will:

  • ask questions that help to uncover underlying problems
  • look for areas of possible compromise
  • ask you and Mary Richards to be flexible

If mediation is successful you'll be asked to sign an agreement to formalise the settlement. If anyone breaks the terms of the agreement the case will go to court.
