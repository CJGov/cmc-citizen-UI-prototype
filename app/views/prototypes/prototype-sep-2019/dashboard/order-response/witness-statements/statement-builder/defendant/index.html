Explain your response with evidence

Tell us what you believe happened and how your evidence proves it.

We'll use your answers to make a witness statement - a document that explains your view of the case to a judge.

Don't worry if you repeat what you've said earlier in your response.

We will ask the following questions to build your witness statement:

  • Is your original response still accurate?
  • What was agreed?
  • What actually happened?
  • Why don't you owe the money?
  • Anything else?

Follow these guidelines:

  • back up your statements by referencing the evidence you have uploaded
  • make sure you use the reference number for each piece of evidence
  • provide sources for any other facts you use
  • try to stick to facts, not opinions
  • avoid using jargon or technical language
