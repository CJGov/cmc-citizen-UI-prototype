What bailiffs can and can't take

Bailiffs can take luxury items, for example a TV or games console.

They cannot take:

  • things the defendents need, such as your clothes, cooker or fridge
  • their work tools and equipment which together are worth less than £1,350
  • someone else’s belongings, such as their partner’s computer
