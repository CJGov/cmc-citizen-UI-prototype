What happens next

Your application for a bailiff service will be sent to the appropriate court. You will receive the letter in the post if it has been issued.

If the bailiff succeeds

If the bailiff succeeds in delivering the application, you'll be given a letter as evidence that the defendant has received the application. This is known as 'Notice of Issue of Warrant of Control'.

If the bailiff can't recover your money

They will return the warrant to the court. We'll contact you to tell you that your application hasn't been served at the address you provided.
