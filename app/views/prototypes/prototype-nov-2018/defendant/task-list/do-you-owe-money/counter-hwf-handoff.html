You can't make a counterclaim using this service

You need to use a paper form if you want help paying your counterclaim fee.

You can either:

  • continue online without making a counterclaim
  • continue online and pay the counterclaim fee yourself
  • submit your response and counterclaim on paper

If you continue online, you would not be able to submit a counterclaim separately, you'd have to create a new claim.
