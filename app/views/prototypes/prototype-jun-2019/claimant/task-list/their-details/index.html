Getting the defendant's details right

It is crucial that you get the defendant's details correct.

So much as a typo in a name, or suing a company by its trading name rather than its registered name, may prevent you from reclaiming your money.

You need to be absolutely sure of:

  • their trading status - whether they are an individual, a sole trader or a company
  • their full, legal name - for companies this may not be the name you know them by
  • their registered or home address - for companies such as shops this is unlikely to be the shop you visited

The best thing to do is speak to the defendant and have them confirm these details in writing.

Otherwise, we can help you find the correct details.
