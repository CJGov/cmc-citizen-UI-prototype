Get the business's full legal name right

Some businesses use a trading name which is different to their full legal name.

Your claim will fail if you use this 'trading name' or any name a company is 'trading as'.

You can check a business's terms and conditions to find its full legal name.

If you find, for example:

  • "Currys PC World is a trading name of DSG Retail Limited" - claim against 'DSG Retail Limited'
  • "TK Maxx is owned and operated by TJX UK" claim against 'TJX UK'
