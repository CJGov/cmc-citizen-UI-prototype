Trading names

Some businesses operate under a trading name which is different to the legal name of the company. You must make the claim against the legal name, not the trading name.

It is best to look at a company's Terms & Conditions to see if there is mention of 'trading as' or 'a trading name of'.

For example:

  • "Currys PC World is a trading name of DSG Retail Limited" - you would make the claim against 'DSG Retail Limited'
  • "TK Maxx is owned and operated by TJX UK" - you would make the claim against 'TJX UK'

Does any paperwork mention a 'trading' name?

Make sure you use the legal name, not the trading name
