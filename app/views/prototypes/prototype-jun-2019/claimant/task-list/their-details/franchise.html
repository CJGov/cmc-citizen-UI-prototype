Franchises

Some business, often food and drink retailers, operate under a franchise model whereby some stores are owned by a small local business rather than the chain itself.

Examples include KFC, McDonalds, Subway, Costa Coffee and Starbucks.

Although two identical stores might be branded 'KFC' one may be owned by 'Kentucky Fried Chicken (Great Britain) Limited' and the other by a franchisee, 'ABC Restaurants Ltd'.

If it a franchise, the operator's business name will normally be on the receipt or displayed in-store. If you are unsure it is best to ask them.

Do you believe the business is a franchise?

You will need to issue your claim in the franchise operator's business name.
