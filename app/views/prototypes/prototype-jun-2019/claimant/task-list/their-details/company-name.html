Enter the business's full legal name

Follow these steps or your claim may fail:

  • make sure you have the correct full legal name - this may not be the name you're familiar with (for example, British Gas is British Gas Trading Limited)
  • don't assume shop or brand names are full legal names - they can be very different (for example, 'ST&H Group Limited' is the full legal name of Saga Holidays)
  • to claim against a franchise, make sure you enter the full legal name of the business that runs the branch, not the chain
  • always include 'ltd', 'limited' or 'plc' if it's part of the business name on paperwork or on the Companies House website

You'll have to pay an extra fee to correct any details later.
