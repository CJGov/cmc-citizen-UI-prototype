Company name

Remember:

  • this must be the full legal name or your claim may fail
  • the full legal name is often different to the name you know the company by. For example, British Gas is 'British Gas Trading Limited'
  • often trading names can be completly differnt to the full legal name. For example CurrysPCWorld is 'DSG Retail Limited'
  • franchises may be run by a different company to the name of the chain.
  • you must nclude 'ltd', 'limited' or 'plc' if shown on paperwork

Make sure you have this correct as you'll have to pay an extra fee to correct it later.
