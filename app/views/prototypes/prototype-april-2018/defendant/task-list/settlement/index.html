Offer a settlement agreement

You can offer a settlement agreement - a legal agreement you sign which describes how you'll pay the money back.

We'll send it to the claimant and they can choose to sign it or issue a County Court Judgment (CCJ) against you.

Even if they accept your repayment plan they can still refuse to sign a settlement agreement. They can issue a CCJ against you instead.

If they sign, the claim is put on hold and they can't issue a CCJ against you unless you break the terms of the agreement.
