Tell us more about your claim

We need some more information in case we need to schedule a hearing.

You'll need to tell us:

  • which court you'd like to a hearing to be at - you can look up your nearest court online (opens in a new window)
  • if you want to hire any experts to give evidence for you, and how much they'll cost
  • if you have any witnesses you want to attend court
  • any dates you, your experts or witnesses can't attend court
  • if you require any special support in court such as an interpreter or wheelchair access

You can come back later and continue this from your account.
