Do you think this claim can be resolved without using an independent expert?

The court doesn't usually need experts for small claims unless the case demands specialist knowledge, for example:

  • in claims about vehicle collisions the court may need an accident reconstruction expert
  • in personal injury claims the court may need a medical expert's report
